The race calendar

In the 2023 season, there were 23 races, the first race of the season was in Bahrain on the 5th of March 2023 and the final race in Abu Dhabi on the 26th of November 2023. The driver who won the worldchamionship of the 2023 season is Max Verstappen who also won the previous year. For the results you can visit the site of Formula 1 with this link. Results

The season of 2024 starts at the 3th of March, the first race will be on 5th of March in Bahrain and will end with the last race in Abu Dabi on the 8th of December 2024. In total there will be 24 races in the season of 2024. For more information about the schedule you can visit the site of Formula 1 with this link. schedule
